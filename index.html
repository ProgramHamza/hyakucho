Project Hyakuchō 百蝶

A wearable, autonomous butterfly swarm platform merging robotic precision with high-fashion aesthetics. One hundred micro-drones launch from an inductive docking harness, tracking and swirling to human gestures in a perfect mechanical dance.

Enter Reveal Explore Concept
Core Subsystems
01 / DOCKING NEST
The Launchpad
An ergonomic, lightweight backpack harness serving as the swarm's nest. Built with brushed aluminum details and voice-coil docking cell bays, featuring inductive fast-charging to ensure continuous swarm readiness.
02 / SWARM ASSEMBLY
Translucent Micro-drones
A flock of 80 to 100 bio-degradable composite butterflies. Features sub-gram electromagnetic actuators and custom wings with 72% translucency that glow soft sakura-pink under flight, creating an organic trail effect.
03 / GESTURE SYNAPSE
Spatial Interaction
Sub-millimeter tracking tracks the wearer's arm and hands. Local edge algorithms translate pointing, sweeping, raising, and closing hands into swarm trajectories, seamlessly blurring the line between body and machine.
