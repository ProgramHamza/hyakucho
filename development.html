Chronicles

Development Timeline

Tracing the development of micro-actuation systems and biological flight middleware from 2021 to 2035.

Phase 01 / 2022

The Relay Calculator

Building a full 4-bit mechanical calculator utilizing electromagnetic relays. This established the basic principles of circuit design and magnetic actuation dynamics.

📁 [ENG-SKETCH // RELAY-CALC-4BIT]
Phase 02 / 2023

Project Anthropomorph

A multi-degree-of-freedom bionic prosthetic hand with electromyography (EMG) control. Developed low-cost motor arrays and skeletal hand enclosures to bridge the gap between neurological signals and robotic joints.

📁 [CAD-RENDER // ANTHROPOMORPH-EMG-V1]
Phase 03 / 2024

RAFUI RF Interface

Designing a radio-frequency (RF) human-machine interface designed for high-interference industrial environments. Evaluated transmission wave propagation, noise filters, and physical wearability.

📁 [PCB-LAYOUT // RAFUI-RF-TRANSCEIVER]
Phase 04 / 2026

Neuromorphic Quadruped (Toyohashi)

R&D Internship at Toyohashi University of Technology under the Artificial Intelligence and Systems Laboratory. Created a Spiking Neural Network (SNN) locomotion middleware for a Unitree Go2 quadruped, evaluating how biological gait adjustments influence human social comfort.

📁 [NEURO-DIAGRAM // SNN-GO2-MIDDLEWARE]
Phase 05 / 2026

Strategic HRI UR5 (NCR)

Developing game-theoretic minimax checkers strategies and computer vision loops under ROS2 at the National Center of Robotics (NaCeRo). Connected strategic neural layers to a UR5 KUKA arm to study complex human-robot cooperative play.

📁 [GRAPH-VIS // COCOHRIP-MINIMAX-CHECKERS]
Phase 06 / 2035

Project HYAKUCHŌ

The culmination of a decade of research. Integrating sub-millimeter computer vision, SNN local boids networks, and inductive magnetic power loops into a high-fashion wearable butterfly swarm launcher.

📁 [3D-CINEMATIC // HYAKUCHŌ-RELEASE-LOOP]
