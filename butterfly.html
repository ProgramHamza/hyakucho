Product Sheet — Micro-UAV

The Butterfly

An engineering marvel weighing under 1.8 grams. Every sub-component is optimized for biological flight patterns and inductive assembly. Scroll to deconstruct and examine the individual elements.

01 / LIFT SYSTEM

Petal-like Wings

Crafted from custom bio-degradable composite layers with 72% light translucency. Engraved with glowing micro-veins serving as flight sensors and reinforcement lines, distributing air currents dynamically.

Material
Translucent Polymer
Translucency
72 % (Sakura pink)
02 / SKELETON

Carbon Fiber Frame

A sub-millimeter carbon truss frame providing ultra-rigid support while maintaining negligible weight. The frame protects the core flight chip during high-speed kinetic formations.

Construction
Sub-millimeter Truss
Weight contribution
0.26 grams
03 / COGNITION

Core ESP32 Logic

Custom miniature microchip running local flight stabilization algorithms and wireless handshake protocols. It processes beacon localization telemetry to orient itself on the fly.

MCU Core
ESP32 Custom Die
Frequency
240 MHz Dual Core
04 / ACTUATION

Voice-Coil Motors

Microscopic voice-coil actuators utilizing high-density magnetic fields. They flap each wing independently at variable angles to perform vertical lifts, sharp banks, and sudden rolls.

Type
Voice-Coil Actuators
Flap Rate
up to 48 Hz
05 / LIFECYCLE

Battery & Contacts

A paper-thin solid-state battery located beneath the main chip. Inductive docking terminals at the bottom clip magnetically to the charging backpack slots, allowing fast, wireless recharging.

Battery Type
Thin Solid-state
Charging Type
Magnetic Inductive
